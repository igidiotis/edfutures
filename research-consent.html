Research Participation Information

Consent to Participate in Research

Purpose

You are invited to participate in a research project collecting speculative stories based on randomly generated elements. This research aims to investigate how educators and educational stakeholders envision the socio-technical implications of AI in future learning environments through a structured scenario-building tool.

What You Will Do

You will receive four random elements from different categories and write a short speculative story incorporating these elements. Your story will be submitted through a Google Form, embedded into the story creation tool.

Data Usage & Privacy

  • Your story will be used for research purposes only
  • No personal identifying information will be collected for the processing of the stories
  • We collect minimal demographic information solely for understanding our tool's audience
  • Text submissions are anonymous; contact information is optional and voluntary
  • Stories may be quoted in research publications and presentations, without any identifying information

Your Rights

Voluntary Participation: Your participation is entirely voluntary. You can choose not to submit your story at any point.

Withdrawal Rights: If you wish to withdraw your submitted story from the research, please contact us with the timestamp of your submission.

Data Security

Stories will be stored securely and accessed only by the research team.

Questions or Concerns?

For any questions about this research, please contact: gidiotis@kth.se

By submitting a story through the tool below, you confirm that you have read this information and consent to participate in this research under these terms.
